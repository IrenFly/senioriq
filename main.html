Welcome!

We are a group for 2SLGBTQ+ identified seniors in the GTA. We celebrate connections within our community and recognize that no elder deserves to go back in the closet.

Our mission is to bring 2SLGBTQ+ identified seniors together for activities and activism, as well as provide information and resources about 2SLBGTQ+ elders to service providers.

Feel free to explore our site and be sure to visit the Membership page to join and get updated on all future events and happenings!
