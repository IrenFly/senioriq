Egale is Canada's leading organization for LGBTQI2S people and issues. Visit their main website to learn about all of the resources they offer. If you would like to read their LGBTQI2S Seniors Community Consult report, visit here
Hamilton Family Health Team is working to create an informed medical community, focusing on LGBTQ health. They can address the full spectrum of health issues faced by 2SLGBTQ+ seniors.
The City of Hamilton has an LGBTQ Advisory Committee. The committee makes recommendations to Council and staff so that the city of Hamilton can provide relevant service to members of the LGBTQ community.
This is an excellent resource for service providers, but also for seniors looking to take control of their healthcare and find providers who specialize in and understand 2SLGBTQ+ issues. For more info about the Rainbow Health Ontario training conference regarding Trans Health, click here.
